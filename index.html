Full Name Mangka Mayanglambam
Daughter Of Mayanglambam Mangangsana Meitei and Maibam Rebika Devi
Address Kakwa Laiphrakpam Leikai, Imphal West 795008.
D.O.B 27th July, 1996
Education M.A. Sociology (2020), Visharad - Manipuri Dance (2015)
Occupation Performer, Researcher and Teacher of Folk music since 2013.
Mangka acquired the knowledge of Manipuri folk music from her father since childhood. She trained in various Manipuri Folk and other art forms; Moirang Sai and Basok from Guru Langathel Thoinu in 2002, Pena from Padmashri Guru Khangembam Mangi in 2006, Khongjom Parva from Guru Khumanthem Sundari in 2009, Hindustani Music from late Guru Mairembam Jiten Singh in 2013, Theatre from Kalakshetra Manipur in 2016, Moirang Sai from Kumbi Inakhunbi in 2019, and Khunung Eshei from Guru Lourembam Bedabati in 2020. She is the youngest ‘A' grade folk artiste of All India Radio Imphal. Also, an empanelment folk artiste of ICCR since 2019. She received the National Child Talent Scholarship, CCRT New Delhi, 2010 to 2016. National Young Talent Scholarship, CCRT New Delhi in the field of Folk Song 2017-19, Approved child artiste of All India Radio, 2008. She has been collaborating with many international artiste (Tokyo, Okinawa, Hawaii, New York, France, Portugal ect) and have been invited and presented her performances on many prestigious occasions on national and international platform
